Why it matters

Every approval is a standing permission to drain you

When you approve a dApp, you often grant unlimited spend rights. If that contract is later exploited or is malicious, it can move every approved token — silently. Approval Guardian is the per-session hygiene check.

🔓

Unlimited allowances

Flags approvals set to the max uint256 — the ones that let a compromised spender take everything.

🕵️

Unverified spenders

Surfaces approvals to contracts not on the trusted-protocol list so you can review before transacting.

🚨

Flagged & malicious

Cross-checks spenders against a curated list of known-exploited addresses and raises risk to CRITICAL.

How it works

One call. Real on-chain evidence.

Discover tokens

Enumerates the ERC-20 tokens a wallet has actually interacted with via Etherscan's free API.

Read live allowances

Reads the current allowance(owner, spender) for each major protocol via RPC — catching the dangerous unlimited approvals.

Score & verdict

Computes a 0–100 safety score, assigns SAFE / REVIEW / AT_RISK / CRITICAL, and returns per-approval evidence.
